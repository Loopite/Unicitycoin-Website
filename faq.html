FAQ

What is Unifycoin ?

Unifycoin is a decentralized Proof Of Stake (POS) cryptocurrency wich his best assets is speed and security.

What is a "cryptocurrency" ?

A cryptocurrency is a digital currency that is secured by cryptography, in contrast to conventional (fiat) currencies where they are secured by a government or cluster of governments.

What is "decentralized" ?

Unifycoin is not run by any individual company or person. All user is a part of the Unifycoin network.

What is "Proof Of Stake (POS)" ?

The Proof of Stake (PoS) concept states that a person can mine or validate block transactions according to how many coins they hold.

I lost my wallet/private key, what can I do?

By prevention, it is advisable to recover your wallet/private key. If you lost your private key, we can do nothing. So, be aware to save it.

How do I back up my wallet?

You need to back up the file "wallet.dat", you can find this in the data directory. On Windows, by default this is "%APPDATA%\Unifycoin\".
